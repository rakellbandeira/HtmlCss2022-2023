Overview

Purpose

My personal website will advertise myself as a Web Developer either to showcase my resume to possible employers or to offer freelancer services. It will expose the main pieces of my portfolio, reinforce the importance and impact of a good design and show how the dynamic of the service works. The website will include information for contact.

Audience

The audience comprises owners of medium to large businesses and their HR team - as it is common for them to intermediate the contact about available jobs or design projects. It's high likely that they will come looking for efficient service and trustworthy skills.

Info about me

My name is Rakell and I’m a full-time UX/UI designer specialised in interfaces with years of experience. I am a specialist in creating minimalist, clean, and high-converting user experiences for web and mobile. I am very passionate about improving webpages / applications design and continuously learning new things to ensure I offer you the best.
